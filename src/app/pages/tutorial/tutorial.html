Welcome to Garbh Vigyan App

"Garbh Vigyan" Mobile app is an all in one app catering needs of pregnant women, doctors, parents, and volunteers. The app will work as a digital mentor for whole family during pregnancy. App will contain health tips, videos, quizzes, recommendations, audios and systematic weekly plans for the users to guide and mentor them on during 280 days. This application will work from Day 1 of pregnancy. This will provide activity and material on day to day basis on this application.

Whats in it for Pregnant Women?

"Garbh Vigyan" is one stop platform which provides health tips, doctor advice through question & answers, diet plan, yoga excercises and much more.

Whats in it for Family Members?

"Garbh Vigyan" app provides a facility to maternal as well as paternal side to interact and understand their roles and responsibilities during the pregnancy period as per the current condition of the pregnant women. It also educates them and helps them understand the science of Garbhsanskar and the role of spiritualism and holistic lifestyle in pregnancy.

Looking for IQ, EQ, SQ & PQ Development?

"Garbh Vigyan" app provides complete, day to day detailed plan to cater the IQ, EQ, SQ, and PQ development of the child in the womb, with the help of videos, quizzes, interactive media and garbh samwad.
