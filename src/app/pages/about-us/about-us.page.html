MEGA SINGING COMPETITION- "SURSHREE"

Rotary globally unites more than a million people

Surshree is one of the biggest singing competitions in India. It is a brand of Rotary District 3040, held annually in Bhopal. Rotary is an international organization of around 1.2 million business professionals and community leaders. Surshree provides a platform for children all over India and has become a popular name among young aspiring singers.

Starting as a school-level competition for participants from grades 9 to 12 (ages 15–21), Surshree has now grown into an international event with global participants. With a unique format of submission through WhatsApp, participants are shortlisted and progress through quarterfinals, semifinals, and the grand finale judged by renowned celebrities like Vinod Rathod, Mahalaxmi Iyer, and Anand Ji.

Season 6 will take place on 05 Dec 2020, with expanded eligibility for participants aged 15–25. Surshree remains dedicated to shaping young musical talents worldwide while maintaining its legacy as a world-class music competition.
